Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is designed to inform users of 'Flash Dodge' (hereinafter referred to as 'the Game') about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information. The Game is provided through the Toss App Service and the Google Play Store.

1. Information Collection

The Game does not directly collect sensitive personal information. However, certain information may be generated or collected during the service provision.

  • In-game Data: High scores, survival time, items owned, etc. (Stored locally on the device using Local Storage)
  • Automatically Collected Information: Advertising Identifiers (ADID/IDFA), service usage records (Collected via third-party SDKs and advertising platforms)

2. Use of Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining game services and records (using local storage)
  • Providing advertising services (personalized ads and tracking)
  • Analyzing service performance and debugging

3. Data Retention and Storage

In principle, the Game does not store personal information on an external server.

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until the app is deleted or browser cache/data is cleared.
  • Advertising IDs: Governed by the policies of the advertising platforms. Users can reset or opt-out of these through device settings.

4. Data Destruction

When a user deletes the app or clears browser data, all game data stored on the device is immediately and permanently destroyed.

5. Third-Party Sharing and Outsourcing

The Game includes SDKs from third-party services to ensure smooth operation. Information may be processed according to their respective policies.

  • Toss (Viva Republica): Provision of leaderboard services and integration of platform features
  • Google Play Services / Google LLC: App installation tracking and integration of Google Play Game Services
  • Google AdMob: Provision of personalized advertisements and performance analysis

6. User Rights and Opt-out

Users have the right to stop using the Game and delete their data at any time. If you do not wish to have your advertising ID collected, you can opt-out through your smartphone's settings (Limit Ad Tracking).
